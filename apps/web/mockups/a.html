Fact Layer

Physics notes

  • PN-01[sample] Surface gravity is 0.166 g — structural loads are dominated by internal pressure, not weight.
  • PN-02[sample] A lunar day–night cycle lasts ~29.5 Earth days; polar siting trades this for near-continuous low-angle sunlight.
  • PN-03[sample] With no atmosphere, micrometeoroid and radiation shielding drives the ~2 m regolith overburden shown on the habitat modules.
  • PN-04[sample] Regolith dust is electrostatically charged and abrasive; airlock and radiator placement in the scene reflects dust-mitigation spacing.
  • PN-05[sample] Round-trip light delay to Earth is ~2.6 s — near-real-time teleoperation of surface rovers remains practical.
